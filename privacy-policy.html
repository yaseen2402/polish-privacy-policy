Privacy Policy for Polish

Last Updated: 1st December 2025

SeaStone ("we," "our," or "us") operates the Polish mobile application (the "App"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of Personal Information when you use our Service.

By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. The Android "Input Method" Warning

Note to Users: When you enable Polish as a keyboard on your Android device, the Android operating system displays a standard warning stating that the keyboard "may be able to collect all the text you type, including personal data like passwords and credit card numbers."

Our Commitment to Privacy: We want to be explicitly clear regarding this warning:

  • Polish does not log, store, or track your keystrokes.
  • We operate as a "passthrough" keyboard.
  • We do not collect passwords, credit card numbers, or sensitive data while you type normally.
  • Data is only collected and processed when you specifically tap the AI-assist buttons (e.g., "Rephrase").

2. Information We Collect

A. Information You Provide (Google Sign-In)

To facilitate account management, we use Google Sign-In. We collect the basic profile information provided by Google to authenticate you:

  • Name
  • Email Address
  • Profile Picture

B. Text Input (Strictly for AI Processing)

We collect text input only when you explicitly engage the AI features.

  • How it works: When you select text and click a button (e.g., "Rephrase"), that specific snippet of text is securely transmitted to the Google Gemini API.
  • Storage: We do not store this text on our servers. It is processed transiently, and the result is returned to your device immediately.

C. Usage Data (Firebase Analytics)

We use Google Firebase Analytics to understand how the app is used. This data is anonymized and aggregated. It includes:

  • Button click counts (e.g., how often "Rephrase" is used).
  • Device model and OS version.
  • Session duration.
  • Note: Firebase does not record the actual text content you type or rephrase.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data for the following purposes:

  • To Provide the Service: Authenticating you via Google and generating AI responses via the Gemini API.
  • To Improve the App: Analyzing Firebase data to identify popular features or performance issues.
  • To Maintain Security: Monitoring for potential abuse or technical errors.

4. Third-Party Service Providers

We utilize specific third-party services to ensure the app functions correctly. We share data with them only to the extent necessary for the app to work.

  • Google Gemini API (Generative AI):
    • Purpose: To process and rephrase your text.
    • Privacy: Your text is sent to Google's servers for processing. Google uses this data in accordance with their API Terms of Service.
  • Google Firebase (Analytics & Auth):
    • Purpose: User authentication and app usage analytics.
    • Privacy: Firebase collects instance IDs and usage metrics.

5. Advertising

We do not display advertisements in the Polish app. We do not use advertising identifiers (Advertising ID) to track your activity across other apps or websites.

6. Data Retention

  • Text Data: We do not retain your text inputs. They are discarded immediately after the AI request is completed.
  • Analytics Data: Usage logs are retained by Firebase for a limited period (typically 2 to 14 months) depending on standard Google Analytics retention settings.
  • Account Data: Your basic Google profile info is retained only as long as you remain signed into the app.
